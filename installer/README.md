# Igdrsil Installer Files

This folder contains files used by the Igdrsil installer.

Most users do not need to open or edit anything here. Use the normal installer for your operating system and follow your organisation's rollout instructions.

## What customers should know

- Igdrsil runs on your organisation's infrastructure.
- The installer does not require you to send documents or chats to Igdrsil.
- If installation fails, contact your internal administrator or Igdrsil support.
